Web Developer Internship

We Develop Personalized Software At OctaNet. We Are Committed To Producing Exceptional Software For Each Of Our Clients. Also we helps the needy student by giving internship into their related fields and domain. This internship will embrace orientation and give emphasis on learning new skills with a deeper understanding of concepts through hands-on application of the knowledge student gained as an intern. This is going to be a great opportunity for the students to enhance their skills and develop themself and make job ready for the future.

Highlights

Benefits of the internship:

This internship provide a hands-on learning experience, allowing interns to apply the knowledge they've gained in the classroom to real-world projects. This practical experience is invaluable for developing technical skills and problem-solving abilities.
Interns have the opportunity to sharpen their web development skills, including programming languages, web frameworks, and development tools. They can gain proficiency in front-end and back-end development, database management, and web design.
Interns can gain insights into the latest trends and emerging technologies in web development, helping them stay updated and competitive in the industry.

Qualifications

  • Basic understanding of web development is essential. You should be familiar with HTML, CSS, and possibly JavaScript
  • Recently graduated or pursuing btech in CS or relevant coursework.
  • Willingness to adapt to the organization's needs. Flexibility and a willingness to take on different tasks can be valuable.
  • Since this is an unpaid internship, it requires more flexibility in terms of hours, make sure you can commit the necessary time to the internship.
